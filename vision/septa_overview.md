### What is Septa?
**Septa** is a research and concept project aimed at designing an  
**autonomous multi-drone ecosystem** built around a centralized Mother Station.

The system focuses on **coordination, environmental awareness, and emergency support**, using modular and scalable architectures.

---

### Why Septa?
Modern infrastructures are fragile during disasters and large-scale disruptions.  
Septa explores how **autonomous aerial systems** can:
- Reduce dependency on fixed infrastructure
- Provide situational awareness in real time
- Support decision-making through centralized intelligence
- Operate as a resilient, adaptive system

---

### Core Principles
- **Centralized control with decentralized agents**
- **Autonomy without loss of accountability**
- **Ethics-first system design**
- **Civilian, humanitarian, and academic focus**
- **Conceptual clarity before physical deployment**

---

### Project Scope
This repository includes:
- High-level system architecture
- Conceptual drone roles
- Use-case scenarios
- Simulations and documentation

This repository **explicitly excludes**:
- Weaponization
- Jamming or destructive capabilities
- Tactical or military deployment instructions
